Welcome to Sean's favourite foods!

Welcome to the page of Sean's favourite foods! In this page, you'll learn the top 5 types of food I like/love!

Food 1:
Pho
A national dish at Vietnam, a chicken based broth with noodles, beef, beef balls, etc.

Food 2:
Dumplings
A bite-sized croissant shaped of dough wrapped around a filling (usually pork) and is cooked to a crisp. They can be sweet or savory — and I love it with a tangy sauce.

Food 3:
Steak
A chunk of meat taken from a cow, depending on the cut of meat will be based on preference. Common cuts of steaks are — Ribeyes, Filet mignon, Sirloin, T-bone, etc. I love my steaks medium rare and more on the rare side, so if they're rare its fine!

Food 4:
Fried Chicken
A part of the chicken with a marinade and batter that gets tossed into huge amounts of oil. The perfection of the crispy bite is immaculate. Any types of fried chicken is amazing, I personally love Churches chicken and also just korean fried chicken.

Food 5:
Sushi and Sashimi
A typical roll of fish with rice and seaweed. My favourite Sushi is california roll and spicy salmon rolls. I also love eating salmon sashimi, the flavour of everything together and the soy sauce is just amazing!
